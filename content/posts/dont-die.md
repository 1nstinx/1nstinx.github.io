## Living Forever In a Sterile Env


As experience of reality becomes more and more digitalized, one can feel a greater and greater amount of the spectrum of human emotion removed from the ailments and dangers of the "real" world.

###
The concept of skin in the game and why we like watching sports.

The heros of our past had to face perils in the "real world" where injury and death are inflicted upon the protagonist in their intense totality.

Wearing armor, having a better metal to craft your weapons out of, tactics of weakening the enemy with cannon fodder so that the real warriors can come in and finish the job, all these facilitate heroic acts that supposedly we all aspire to.

I would argue that these faclitating implements are now pointed inwards, in that our heros no longer take into battle with skin in the game as such, but rather a full life is now more and more possible within virtual worlds.

The capacity for abstraction has us feeling fulfilled by experiences which objectively are just staring a screen.

The transformatin of what and/or who we strive to be has changed.
