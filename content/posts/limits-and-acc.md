## The Limits to Growth: A Systems View of Civilization

In 1972, at the commissioning of The Club of Rome *-TO BE SOMEHOW EXPLAINED WHO THEY ARE-*, MIT's Systems Dynamics Group plotted out a report, which came to be known as 'The Limits to Growth'.
This report is a model of the long-term interactions between the following factors:
1. Global Population;
2. Industrial Output;
3. Food Production;
4. Nonrenewable Resources;
5. Pollution;

All of these factors were then plugged into the 'World3' computer model *-TO BE SOMEHOW EXPLAINED WHAT IT IS-*, which simulated dozens of scenarios.
